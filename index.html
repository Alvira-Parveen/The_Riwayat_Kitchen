The story
Made with a mother's love
"Riwayat" — the stories, traditions and flavours that live on through generations.

Every great meal has a beginning, and ours began in a home where food was the language of love. Our mother has always believed that the best recipes aren't rushed ; they're nurtured with patience, tradition and care. Friends would ask for her recipes. Relatives would request her biryani. Neighbours would wait for special occasions just to enjoy her cooking.

She never imagined those recipes would one day reach thousands through social media or become the foundation of her own kitchen.

Riwayat Kitchen was born from that dream — a cloud kitchen dedicated to serving authentic, comforting food that feels just like home. It carries those same recipes, the same care, and the same promise: food that comforts, connects, and creates memories around every table.

Every order is freshly prepared, carefully packed and sent with the warmth we'd serve our own family.
